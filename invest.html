  • A system that turns fragmented gigs into scalable, repeatable transactions
Why It Scales
  • Strong network effects between businesses and creative talent
  • Repeat usage driven by ongoing demand for experiences
  • Expansion across cities, categories, and industries
The Vision

  • Move the industry from informal transactions to structured systems
  • Build the default platform for experience-driven collaboration
